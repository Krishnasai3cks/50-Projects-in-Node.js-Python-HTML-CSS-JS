Rules

  • 1. Gather the green color boxes
  • 2. Use up down left arrow keys to navigate your player
  • 3. Your player color is white
  • 4. The food color is green
  • 5. The red color blocks are blockades
  • 6. You have to collect all the five blocks with small letters first.
  • 7. After collecting small letter blocks you can collect capital letter blocks.
  • 8. If you collect the capital block before collection all the smaller blocks first then you lose.
